FAQ

Fair questions.

How big can my lists be?

No limit. A 250,000-row list is fine — big scans run in the background and we email you when your results are ready.

Where does the data come from?

Directly from Amazon's Selling Partner API through your own authorized seller account — the same data Amazon shows sellers, in real time.

Why is this so much cheaper than the tools I've tried?

Because scans run through your own Amazon seller authorization, our costs don't grow with your usage — so we don't need tiers, credits, or caps. And because we skip the fluff features that don't change a buying decision.

Do you support marketplaces outside Amazon US?

Not at launch. US first, done properly.

What happens to my uploaded lists?

Processed, then the raw file is deleted. Results stay in your account until you delete them. We never share or aggregate customer data.
